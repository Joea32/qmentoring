Our Founder

Patricia Lungo

With a distinguished career at Hillingdon Council, Patricia Lungo has become a beacon of hope and empowerment for communities navigating complex social challenges. Armed with a Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Criminology and Psychology, Patricia masterfully combines rigorous academic insight with years of hands-on experience to drive transformative, meaningful change.

Patricia’s passion extends far beyond her professional accomplishments. For years, she has devoted herself to social work volunteering—standing alongside vulnerable individuals and families with unwavering compassion and dedication.

Among Patricia’s most impactful contributions is her leadership of a groundbreaking community outreach program during one of the toughest periods faced by local residents. This initiative not only delivered vital resources but also rekindled hope and resilience, creating a lasting legacy of support and solidarity.

Today, Patricia continues to champion community empowerment through mentorship and advocacy, inspiring others to carry forward her vision of a world where every individual is supported, valued, and heard.
